Vision

The vision of the sports committee is to organize training, coaching, and education in sports for everyone to stay physically fit. It serves the interests of students in competitive sports and recreational activities.

Mission

The committee aims to enhance participants' interest in sports. We plan to organize competitions in various sports: Walkathon, Football, Cricket, Basketball, Volleyball, Kabaddi, Chess, and Badminton.

Objectives

The sports committee promotes health, well-being, and physical skills among students. It aspires to inculcate qualities like sportsmanship, team spirit, and bonding.
